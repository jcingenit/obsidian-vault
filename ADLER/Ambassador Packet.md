# 1) What Is Adler?

Adler is a **digital mental health platform** designed to help people **relieve negative feelings** through guided reflection and science-driven insights.

Adler reinvents traditional journaling by combining:

- **Guided emotional reflection**
- **Fast, science-driven analytical insights**
- **Deep physical & emotional relaxation exercises**

Each session helps users:

- Reflect on what they feel  
- Experience emotions safely and fully  
- Learn from emotional patterns  
- Reduce the intensity and impact of negative feelings  

## Adler’s Purpose

Adler helps users experience **fast emotional and physical relief** from negative experiences — while saving time and money compared to traditional options.

We focus on both:

✅ **Immediate relief**  
✅ **Long-term emotional skill-building**  

**Not one without the other.**

# 2) What Adler Is — and Isn’t

### Adler *is*:
- A supportive mental health tool  
- A guided emotional reflection experience  
- A calm, nonjudgmental space to process emotions  
- A complement to therapy or professional care  

### Adler is *not*:
- Therapy  
- A medical or clinical treatment  
- A crisis or emergency service  
- A replacement for licensed mental health professionals  

**Important:** As a partner, you must never position Adler as a **cure**, **diagnosis**, or **guaranteed solution**.

# 10) Campaign Commitment

### Additional Campaign Commitment Terms & Ambassador Expectations

To keep the Adler Ambassador Program impactful, consistent, and aligned with our mission, all ambassadors are expected to meet the following participation standards:
#### Content Delivery & Timeline
- Ambassadors must publish **at least one (1)** promotional deliverable within **7 days** of receiving confirmation of enrollment and their affiliate tracking link.
- Deliverables should be posted on an approved channel (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, blog, newsletter, etc.) and must include a **clear call-to-action** directing users to Adler via your link.
#### Content Quality Standards
To ensure messages remain ethical and trustworthy, ambassadors agree that all content will:
- Reflect Adler accurately and responsibly
- Use **non-clinical, non-medical language**
- Avoid exaggerated claims or “instant fix” messaging
- Emphasize emotional relatability and healthy self-reflection
- Avoid harmful, graphic, or triggering content and sensationalized mental health narratives
#### Brand & Disclosure Requirements
- Ambassadors must disclose their relationship to Adler in promotional content using clear language such as:
  - “Adler Ambassador”
  - “Partner”
  - “Affiliate”
  - “Sponsored” (if applicable)
- Disclosures must be **visible** (not hidden in a long caption) and follow the rules of the platform you are posting on.
#### Communication & Confirmation
- Ambassadors must provide Adler with a link or screenshot of the posted content within **48 hours of publishing** (or as otherwise requested).
- Ambassadors are expected to maintain responsive communication and reply to program-related messages within a reasonable timeframe (typically **2–3 business days**).
#### Consistency & Participation
- Adler may offer optional campaign waves, bonus incentives, or special challenges. Participation is not required unless explicitly stated.
- If an ambassador becomes inactive for **30+ days** without communication, Adler reserves the right to pause or remove the ambassador from the program.
#### Misrepresentation & Removal
Adler reserves the right to immediately revoke ambassador status if content includes:
- Medical/clinical misinformation
- Harmful or exploitative mental health messaging
- Hate speech, harassment, threats, or discrimination
- False claims about Adler’s features or outcomes
- Any behavior that may compromise the safety of the community or reputation of the Adler mission
By continuing in the program, you agree to represent Adler in a way that prioritizes **integrity, emotional safety, and user wellbeing**.
