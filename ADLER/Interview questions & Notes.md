### **ADLER DESCRIPTION**
Adler is a digital mental health platform. It allows you to reflect on what you feel and get science-driven analytical insights from your sessions. Adler combines traditional psychology methods with a guided mental exercise, focusing on breathing to drive psychological relief from negative feelings and reinforce the effect of positive emotional experiences.

Our solution for driving emotional balance is based on a set of mental exercises that involve self-reflection, physical and emotional relaxation, and guided breathing to help you understand what you feel, get relief from negative feelings, and offer simple strategies on emotional coping to enhance the effects of the positive emotions you experience.
