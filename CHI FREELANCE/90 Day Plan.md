# First 90-day action plan

## Days 1–7: Foundation

- Pick 2–3 target niches.
- Decide your packages.
- Build your own website.
- Create a simple logo/brand.
- Write your service pages.
- Create a Calendly link.
- Create a proposal template.
- Create a contract template.
- Set up Stripe or invoicing.
- Make a Google Sheet or CRM for leads.

### Deliverable by end of week 1

A live portfolio site with:

- Homepage
- Services
- Portfolio
- About
- Contact

---

## Days 8–21: Portfolio and audit engine

- Build 3 sample projects.
- Create a one-page audit template.
- Audit 15 Chicago businesses.
- Send 10–15 personalized emails.
- Post on LinkedIn that you’re accepting freelance web clients.
- Message Purdue alumni in Chicago.
- Join 3–5 local business/tech groups.

### Deliverable by end of week 3

- 3 portfolio examples
- 15 business audits
- First batch of outreach sent

---

## Days 22–45: Local networking

- Attend 2–4 events.
- Reach out to 100 businesses.
- Offer 10 free audits.
- Ask for 10 referral intros.
- Post weekly on LinkedIn.
- Follow 100 local businesses on Instagram.
- Start commenting thoughtfully on local business posts.

### Goal by day 45

- 3–5 discovery calls
- 1 paid client
- 1 testimonial or case study in progress

---

## Days 46–75: Close and deliver

- Finish first client project.
- Ask for testimonial.
- Create a case study.
- Raise prices slightly.
- Offer maintenance plan.
- Ask client for 2 referrals.
- Continue outreach.

### Goal by day 75

- 2–3 paid projects completed or booked
- 1 recurring maintenance client
- Stronger portfolio

---

## Days 76–90: Build repeatability

- Refine your offer.
- Focus on the niche that responded best.
- Create a lead magnet: “Chicago Small Business Website Checklist.”
- Build a referral system.
- Reach out to agencies for overflow work.
- Build partnerships with photographers, copywriters, designers, and marketers.

### Goal by day 90

- 3–5 clients
- 1–3 monthly retainers
- Clear niche direction
