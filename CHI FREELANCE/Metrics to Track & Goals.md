# 23. Metrics to track

Use a simple spreadsheet.

Track:

- Business name
- Niche
- Neighborhood
- Website
- Contact name
- Email/Instagram
- Date contacted
- Follow-up date
- Status
- Pain point
- Offer sent
- Call booked
- Proposal amount
- Closed/lost
- Notes

## Weekly goals

- 50 new leads
- 25 emails
- 10 social messages
- 5 follow-ups
- 3 audits
- 1 event
- 1–2 discovery calls

If you do this consistently, you will get conversations.
