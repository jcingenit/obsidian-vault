# 14. Contract essentials

Always use a contract.

Include:

- Scope of work
- Timeline
- Payment schedule
- Revision limits
- Late payment terms
- Ownership terms
- Cancellation terms
- Client responsibilities
- What happens if client delays feedback
- Maintenance terms
- Hosting/domain responsibility
- Portfolio usage rights

### Important boundary

Do not promise results like “I guarantee you’ll get 50 leads.” Instead say:

> The website will be built using conversion-focused best practices designed to improve clarity, usability, and lead generation.
