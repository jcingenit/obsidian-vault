# 9. Offer a free website audit

This should be your main entry offer.

## The audit should include

- First impression
- Mobile experience
- Speed/performance notes
- CTA clarity
- SEO basics
- Trust signals
- Booking/contact flow
- 3–5 recommended fixes

Keep it short. One page is enough.

### Audit title

> Quick Website Review for [Business Name]

### Audit structure

**1. What’s working**

Start positive.

**2. What may be costing you leads**

Be specific.

**3. Quick wins**

Give practical fixes.

**4. How I’d improve it**

Mention your service.

**5. CTA**

> I’d be happy to walk through this with you and show what a refreshed version could look like.
