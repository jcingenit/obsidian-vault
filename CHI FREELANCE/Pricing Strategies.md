# 12. Pricing strategy

Do not undercharge too much just because you are starting. You can offer “founding client” pricing, but frame it properly.

## Starting prices

|Service|Starter price|
|---|---|
|Landing page|$500–$1,250|
|Website refresh|$750–$1,500|
|Full small business website|$1,500–$3,500|
|Monthly care plan|$100–$300/month|
|Hourly updates|$50–$100/hour|

### Best pricing model

Use project pricing, not hourly pricing.

Hourly pricing punishes you for getting faster. Project pricing lets the client understand the full investment.

## Deposit structure

Use:

- 50% upfront
- 25% after first draft
- 25% before launch

For smaller projects under $750, you can do 50% upfront and 50% before launch.
