# 15. Client process

A polished process makes you seem more experienced.

## Step-by-step client workflow

### Step 1: Discovery call

Ask:

- What does your business do?
- Who is your ideal customer?
- What is not working with the current website?
- What action do you want visitors to take?
- Do you need calls, bookings, orders, inquiries, or email signups?
- Do you have brand assets?
- Who are your competitors?
- What is your timeline?
- What is your budget?

---

### Step 2: Proposal

Send within 24 hours after the call.

---

### Step 3: Deposit and contract

No work starts until both are complete.

---

### Step 4: Content collection

Get:

- Logo
- Brand colors
- Photos
- Services/menu
- Pricing/packages
- Contact info
- Social links
- Testimonials
- Business hours
- FAQs
- Booking/order links

---

### Step 5: Sitemap and wireframe

Show page structure before designing.

---

### Step 6: First draft

Send a preview link.

---

### Step 7: Revisions

Limit revisions clearly.

Example:

> This project includes two rounds of revisions.

---

### Step 8: Launch

Before launch, check:

- Mobile
- Forms
- Links
- SEO titles
- Meta descriptions
- Page speed
- Analytics
- Domain
- SSL
- Contact info
- 404 page
- Favicon

---

### Step 9: Handoff

Give client:

- Login info
- Basic editing instructions
- Maintenance plan offer
- 30-day support window
- Testimonial request
