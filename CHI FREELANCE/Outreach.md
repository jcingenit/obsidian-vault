# 10. Outreach scripts

## Cold email script

**Subject:** Quick website idea for [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

I’m a Chicago-based website developer and came across [Business Name] while looking through local businesses in [Neighborhood].

I liked [specific thing: menu, brand, reviews, photos, concept], but I noticed a few website improvements that could make it easier for customers to [book / call / order / inquire], especially on mobile.

A few quick ideas:

- [Specific improvement #1]
- [Specific improvement #2]
- [Specific improvement #3]

I help local businesses build clean, mobile-friendly websites that are designed around getting more leads, bookings, and customers.

Would you be open to a quick website review? I can send over a few notes, no pressure.

Best,  
Jackson
