**Client:** Melissa Hathaway ("Client")

and

**Service Provider:** Jackson Ingenito ("Developer")

Client and Developer may be referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

---

## 1. PURPOSE OF AGREEMENT

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a professional working relationship whereby Developer will provide website-related services to improve, enhance, and maintain Client’s existing Squarespace website. Further work will also be done in regard to managed social media platforms, upon the consent of both Parties. The Parties acknowledge that the goal of this collaboration is to better represent the quality of the property, improve user experience, and support increased direct bookings and overall performance.

This Agreement reflects an ongoing collaboration and may evolve over time as Client’s needs and priorities change.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES (GENERAL)

Developer will provide website design, development, configuration, and advisory services as reasonably requested by Client and mutually agreed upon by the Parties. These services may include, but are not limited to:

- Website design and layout enhancements
- Functionality improvements related to bookings and payments
- Integration or coordination with third-party tools and platforms
- Content updates and structural improvements
- General website optimization and troubleshooting

Specific tasks, features, or enhancements may be discussed verbally or in writing (email or message) and incorporated into the ongoing collaboration without requiring a formal amendment to this Agreement, unless otherwise stated.

---

## 3. PLATFORM & THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Client acknowledges that the website operates on Squarespace and may be connected to third-party services such as booking engines, channel managers, pricing tools, review platforms, or payment processors.

Developer will make reasonable efforts to work within the technical limitations of these platforms. Developer is not responsible for outages, errors, pricing discrepancies, data sync issues, or policy enforcement caused by third-party services.

Client agrees to provide necessary access credentials, permissions, documentation, and timely responses as needed for Developer to perform services.

---

## 4. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

- Provide accurate content, policies, documents, and information
- Review work and provide feedback in a timely manner
- Maintain ownership and responsibility for business decisions, pricing, cancellation policies, and legal compliance
- Ensure that all materials provided (including rental agreements, images, and reviews) are owned by or licensed to Client

---

## 5. COMPENSATION

Compensation for services shall be as follows:

- **Rate / Fee Structure:** $65/hour
- **Estimated Cost:** $1,300 - $1,950 for initial website development and implementation
- **Payment Schedule:** Weekly/Bi-Weekly (as decided by the Client)

Any additional work outside the originally discussed scope may be billed separately upon Client approval.
